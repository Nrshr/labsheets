Frequently Asked Qustions(FAQs)


Are KhEC & KhCE same?
No, Khwopa Engineering College(KhEC) is affiliated to Purbanchal University & Khwopa College of Engineering(KhCE) is affiliated to Tribhuvan University.

When can deposit be refunded
Deposits can be refunded only after passing out from the college.

Does college provide any scholarship?
College provides full or partial scholarships for diligent and poor students. It also provides scholarships on the basis of academic performance of each semester.
